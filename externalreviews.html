Reviewer

Description

IGN is a worldwide known video game and media website known across the world. They are also known for their reviews on many games. They were founded in 1996 by Chris Anderson.

Destructoid is a website that was originally a video-game blog founded by Yanier Gonzalez in 2006. It's known to produce video game news and stories. It was taken over by Enthusiast Gaming in 2017. Their mascot, Mr Destructoid has featured in many PC and console games.

Game Informer is a monthly video game magazine in America that contains reviews, news and articles of video games. It was founded in 1991 by FuncoLand and is currently owned by GameStop Corp. Game Informer also posts their reviews on their website.

Metacritic is a website which has a score for each game based on other reviewers. They have links to these other reviewers and are essentially similar to RottenTomatoes (a film/tv based version of it). It was founded in 1999.

Nintendo Life is a website focussed on news and reviews of Nintendo products including video games. It was founded in 2005 by Nlife Media.

Gamespot is a website that provides news, information and reviews on many games. It was founded in 1996 when some employees left IDG and founded Spotmedia Communications. It is one of the most renown game reviewing platform in the world.

Christ Centered Gamer is a reviewing site that includes very clear reviews and questions the ethics of some reviewing sites. It puts two scores for each game: one for technical (gameplay, controls, etc); and one for morality (language, gore, etc). It was founded in 2007.
